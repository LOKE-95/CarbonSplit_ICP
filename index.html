About CarbonSplit

How It Works

CarbonSplit is a revolutionary platform that rewards climate action. When you submit proof of eco-friendly actions, our AI verifies your contribution and calculates carbon credits. These credits are split 50/50 - half for you to use in our NFT marketplace, half permanently locked for Earth's benefit.

AI Verification

Every action requires photo proof that our advanced AI analyzes for authenticity. This ensures all contributions are genuine and prevents fraud, maintaining the integrity of our climate impact tracking.

Blockchain Storage

All verified actions are permanently stored on the Internet Computer (ICP) blockchain, creating an immutable, transparent record of climate contributions that can never be altered or deleted.

Monthly Quests

Compete with other climate warriors in monthly quests. The top 3 contributors earn exclusive NFT rewards and special recognition in our community.

Ranking System

Progress through 5 ranks (Bronze to Diamond) based on your climate impact. Higher ranks unlock exclusive NFTs, discounts, and special marketplace privileges.

NFT Marketplace

Use your earned carbon credits to purchase exclusive NFTs. Each rank unlocks new collections, with higher ranks getting better discounts and access to rare items.
